# CEO Briefing Skill

**Name:** ceo-briefing
**Tier:** Silver Capstone (L11)
**Trigger:** Weekly (Sunday 11 PM via cron) or manual invocation

---

## Purpose

Generate a comprehensive Monday Morning CEO Briefing by cross-referencing business goals, completed tasks, activity logs, and subscription data.

---

## Data Sources

| Source | Path | What to Extract |
|--------|------|-----------------|
| Business Goals | `/Business_Goals.md` | Revenue targets, KPIs, subscription audit rules |
| Completed Tasks | `/Done/*.md` | Task names, completion dates, durations, revenue |
| Activity Logs | `/Logs/*.json` | Events from the reporting period |
| Subscriptions | `/Business_Goals.md` → Active Subscriptions | Cost, last activity, renewal dates |

---

## Reporting Period

- **Period:** Previous 7 days (Monday through Sunday)
- **Generated:** Sunday night for Monday morning review

---

## Analysis Steps

### Step 1: Revenue Analysis
- Sum `revenue` fields from completed tasks in `/Done/` for the reporting period
- Compare against weekly target ($2,500) and monthly target ($10,000)
- Calculate percentage of monthly goal achieved
- Identify top revenue-generating tasks

### Step 2: Task Completion Analysis
- Count tasks completed vs. tasks received during the period
- Calculate completion rate (target: >90%)
- List any tasks still stuck in pipeline (Needs_Action, Pending_Approval)
- Flag tasks that exceeded their expected duration

### Step 3: Bottleneck Detection
- For each completed task with `expected_duration` and `actual_duration`:
  - Calculate ratio: actual / expected
  - Apply severity: 1.5x = Minor, 2.0x = Moderate, 3.0x+ = Critical
  - Include delay amount and likely cause
- Flag any pipeline stage with growing queue counts

### Step 4: Subscription Audit
- Check each subscription against last activity date
- Flag inactive (30+ days): warning
- Flag inactive (60+ days): recommend cancellation
- Calculate potential monthly savings from cancellations
- Note upcoming renewal dates (within 14 days)

### Step 5: Proactive Suggestions
- Cost optimization recommendations based on subscription audit
- Upcoming deadline tracking from active tasks
- Process improvement insights from bottleneck patterns
- Revenue acceleration opportunities
